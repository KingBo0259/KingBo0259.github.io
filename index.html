Frequently Asked Questions

We've compiled a list of the most common questions and solutions to help you quickly resolve issues

You can update the App through the following methods:

  • App Store (iOS): Open the App Store → Tap on your profile icon → Find our App → Tap Update
  • Google Play (Android): Open the Google Play Store → Tap the menu button → My apps & games → Find our App → Tap Update
  • Automatic Updates: You can also enable automatic updates in the app store settings to ensure your App is always up to date

If you're unable to log in, try the following steps:

  1. Check your network connection and try reconnecting to Wi-Fi or switching to mobile data
  2. Confirm that the username/email and password you entered are correct, paying attention to case sensitivity and special characters
  3. Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" feature
  4. Clear the App cache data (Settings → Application Management → Find our App → Clear Cache)
  5. If none of the above methods work, please contact our customer support team

Our App provides both automatic and manual data backup options:

Automatic Backup:

Enable the "Automatic Backup" feature in Settings, and the App will automatically back up your data to our servers when you're connected to Wi-Fi and your device has sufficient battery

Manual Backup:

Tap Settings → Backup & Restore → Backup Now to manually create a data backup

Restore Data:

After logging into your account on a new device, the App will automatically prompt you to restore data, or you can go to Settings → Backup & Restore → Select a backup version to restore

If the App is running slowly, you can try the following optimization methods:

  • Close other running applications to free up system resources
  • Clear the App cache data (Settings → Application Management → Find our App → Clear Cache)
  • Ensure your device has sufficient storage space. We recommend having at least 2GB of free space
  • Update the App to the latest version, as new versions often include performance optimizations
  • Restart your device. Sometimes a simple restart can resolve many performance issues
  • If the problem persists, check if your device meets the minimum system requirements for the App

To cancel your subscription or request a refund, follow these steps:

Cancel Subscription:

iOS: Settings → Your Account → Subscriptions → Find our App → Cancel Subscription

Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions → Find our App → Cancel Subscription

Note that after canceling, you can still use the service until the end of your current billing period

Request a Refund:

If you're dissatisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 48 hours of purchase via:

iOS: App Store → Account → Purchased → Find our App → Report a Problem → Select Refund Reason

Android: Google Play Store → Menu → Order History → Find our App → Request a Refund

For refund requests超过 48 hours, please contact our customer support team to explain the situation
